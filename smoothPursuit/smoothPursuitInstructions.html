Smooth Pursuit

Instructions: In this challenge, you will see a moving red target. Focus on the target as closely as possible with your eyes and follow it as it moves. Try to keep your head still.

Use Cases: Smooth pursuit tests are used to guage both cognitive function and motor control. Many conditions disrupt the neural pathways that guide smooth pursuit, including neurodegenerative diseases (e.g., Alzheimer's, Parkinson's), neuropsychiatric disorders (e.g. ADHD, autism), and acute conditions (e.g., drug and alcohol use).
